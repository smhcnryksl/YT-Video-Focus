# YT Video-Focus
Extension for chrome based web browsers to make you focus only on the video!


INTRODUCTION:
Do you find yourself constantly distracted while studying, researching, or working on YouTube? "YT Video Focus" is here to help! This extension gives you complete control over your YouTube environment, allowing you to stay laser-focused on your goals.
